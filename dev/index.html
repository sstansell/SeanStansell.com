ABOUT ME

WEB
DEVELOPER

With well over 15 years experience developing websites, there are few technologies I haven't used. While there languages I like more than others, I'm not a zealot. I'm more of a "right tool for the job" kind of guy. Trends will come and go, but solid principles will never go out of style. So whether a job calls for PHP or .NET, Ruby or Python, NodeJS or plain old HTML, my experience and base of solid fundamentals get the job done.

PROJECT
MANAGER

Keeping a complicated project moving forward, while keeping costs in check is a delicate art. It requires knowledge of good processes, and knowing how to adopt those processes to your situation. As a Scrum Alliance® certified ScrumMaster, and practicing project manager for more than 10 years, I have both the training and hands on experiece to keep teams moving in the right direction.

PRODUCT
MANAGER

"If you build it, they will come" was great in the movies, but the real world is more complicated than that. Translating an idea into a successful product takes discipline, research, and a willingness to learn from failures. Managing development, market research, operations, support, and sales is a delicate balance. With 5 years of Product Management experience, a Scrum Alliance® Scrum Product Owner certification, and a certification in the Pragmatic Marketing framework, I have the experience and training to make a product successful.
